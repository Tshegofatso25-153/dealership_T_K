About Us

  1. Introduction/Mission

    2. At T_K Dealership, we are passionate about connecting people with vehicles that match their lifestyle. Our mission is to provide reliable and affordable service, transparent deals, and a customer-first experience.

    We are committed to offering vehicles that meet the highest standards of safety and performance to our customers.

    Guided by our values of honesty and excellence, we aim to build lasting relationships and contribute to a safer, more connected driving community.

  2. Company Background

    3. T_K Dealership was founded in 2010 by a group of automotive enthusiasts who wanted to create a dealership that prioritized customer satisfaction and quality vehicles. Over the years, we have grown into a large company with trusted name in the automotive industry, serving customers across Botswana and beyond.

    Our dealership is located in Mogoditshane, Gaborone, and we have built a reputation for offering a wide selection of vehicles, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service. We are proud to be a part of the local community and are dedicated to providing our customers with the best car buying experience possible.

  3. Company Values

    4. At T_K Dealership, we are guided by core values that shape our approach to business and customer service. These values include integrity, customer focus, quality, and innovation.

    • Integrity - Honest and transparent dealings.
    • Quality - Vehicles and services that meet high standards.
    • Customer Care -Putting clients first in every interaction.
    • Innovation -Adapting to new technologies and trends.
    • Community - Supporting local initiatives and building trust.
  4. Services

    5. At T_K Dealership, we pride ourselves on being more than just a dealership. Our services cover every aspect of car ownership—from helping you select the right vehicle, to offering flexible financing, to providing expert maintenance and repair.

    We also assist with insurance and registration, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience. Whatever your automotive needs, our team is here to deliver quality, reliability, and peace of mind.
